Home

Welcome to the Sun & Stone community, where every piece tells a story of European charm and enduring quality. Our collections are thoughtfully curated for those who cherish the legacy of furniture designed to withstand the test of time. We believe in cultivating sanctuaries, with each item capturing the warmth of sunlit interiors and timeless beauty of rustic European craftsmanship. Perfect for homeowner and designers alike, Sun & Stone invites you to explore a selection where history meets modern living - furniture where light meets legacy.

Buying from us?

When you purchase from us, our team ensures that each detail—from the selection process to delivery—is seamless and tailored to meet your needs. We take pride in offering a personalized shopping experience, with support available to help you find the perfect fit for your space. Once in your home, these pieces transform your space with a warmth and elegance that speaks to generations past while enriching your everyday life.

Depending on shipping location your unique piece may arrive anytime between 1-4 weeks

Working with us?

Working with a Sun & Stone designer is a seamless journey to create a space that reflects your unique style and values. It begins with an in-depth consultation—either in person or online—where we learn your vision and design needs. From there, our designer curates a selection of pieces that blend warmth, heritage, and functionality, refining each detail with your input.

We keep you updated throughout, providing visualizations to help you picture how each piece will fit. Once selections are set, we take care of delivery and installation, ensuring every item finds its perfect place. The result is a home filled with timeless elegance—a true sanctuary where light meets legacy.
